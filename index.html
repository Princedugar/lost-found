About This Website

College Lost & Found Portal

Find your lost items or help others by reporting found items.

Unknown Registry is a simple College Lost & Found Portal created to help students easily report and find lost items inside the campus.

Students can report lost items, upload images, and contact the person who found them.

This project is created for college use to make the lost and found process simple and fast.
